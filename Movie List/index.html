The Best Movies according to Saransh Upadhyay

My top 3 movies of all-time

Light Shop

Light Shop is a movie about how a soul lingers in the after world while a person is in coma

Transformers

Transformers is a movie about how a car transforms into a robot and fights with other robots

Drive

Drive is a movie about a stuntman who drives for criminals and gets caught in a heist
